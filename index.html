ABOUT US

Thai food ranks as one of the world's great culinary arts. Today Thai food is found around the world and the cuisine has earned a deserved reputation for its creativity, variety and of course,superb taste.


We are proud to present you exotic cuisine from Thailand. We are offering a varied menu where you can choose everything from delicious appetizers,curries, stir-fried dishes,rice, noodles and desserts,each will be professionally prepared by a Thai origin chef.


Our services include our team of talented chefs cooking live at your function as well as food drop offs at your home & specializes in catering for private and corporate events. Our team of chefs, bartenders and wait staff are spirited foodies who love to share ideas that will elevate your event and make it stand out! Their attention to detail will ensure you have a hassle free experience from start to finish. And our extensive menus, with Thai, Asian, Indian, Italian, Mexican, Mediterranean fare, are so current that your guests will be talking about them for days.


So if you’re looking for a Thai delivery or catering company that’s just right, we’ve got you covered
